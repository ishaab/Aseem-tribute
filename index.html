In Loving Memory of Aseem

Gone Too Soon Due to AIDS

Aseem was a truly good soul — kind-hearted, always there for his friends, with a sense of humour that… well, let’s say his bad jokes were memorable in their own way.

His passing reminds us how fragile life is, and how important it is to talk openly about illnesses like AIDS without stigma.

Rest easy, Aseem. You’ll be missed, but never forgotten.
